Simple Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Remember those delicious Mac and Cheese meals from when your were a kid? ... When the boxed stuff and over priced selections extravagant cheese didn't ruin it ?

Well this recipe will do the trick! Quick ,simple and delicious , just like how it should be. Just like the good old days.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) box elbow macaroni
  • ¼ cup butter
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the macaroni. Boil for 8 min , double the amount of paste to make it thicker
  2. Make thickener.The base of the dish is created here. First melt the butter then add the lour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth. Slowly pour the milk and stir.
  3. And lastly , add in cheese and the macaroni.Add the cheese and stir until smoothly melted! Then... the paste

Enjoy!
