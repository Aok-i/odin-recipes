Folded Gimbap

Description

Love Korean gimbap but have trouble rolling it up? Well , how about folding it . Makes for a perfect and simple lunch

No cutting required and eat it like a good old sandwich

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup cooked rice
  • 1/4 teaspoon sesame oil
  • salt to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon sesame seeds -- optional
  • 1 gim (김밥용 김), seaweed sheet (aka laver, nori)

Protein

  • 2 thin slices Spam or 2 tablespoons canned tuna (press out the liquid)
  • 1 fried egg (no runny yolk)

Vegetables

  • 1 lettuce leaf
  • 1/2 small cucumber or carrot

Steps

  1. Mix in salt and the sesame oil in warm rice. Add in sesame seeds if preferred
  2. If using Spam, pan fry over medium heat for a minute or two
  3. Thinly slice the cucumber or carrot
  4. Take a sheet of nori , the shiny side facing down and cut a slit in the middle of the sheet from the short side
  5. Spread rice on the bottom left quarter , and then arrange the other ingredients on each of the other quarters. Fold the bottom left quarter over the top left , then to the top right and lastly , fold downwards

Notes

  1. Squeeze out any liquid first from the ingredients
  2. Pat dry vegetables with papertowel
  3. Do not overload it
  4. Place small pieces or easy to fall out ingredients next to the rice one

Enjoy!
