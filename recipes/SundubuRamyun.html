Sundubu Ramyun

Description

Ever watch a Korean drama and after all the emotion ... you just crave for some instant noodles "RAMYUN" ? Well , why not add some extra ingredients to spice it up . Some soft tofu , some Sundubu !!!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 package sundubu, 순두부
  • 1/2 package spicy ramyun, 라면 or your favorite
  • 1 tablespoon chopped scallion
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/2 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon gochugaru, 고추가루 - optional
  • 1 egg
  • pinch pepper

Steps

  1. Put the sesame oil, garlic and 1/2 of the ramyun seasoning mix and the gochugaru (optional) into a small pot and mix over a medium heat
  2. Add 1 cup of water and turn the heat up to medium high. When the water has started to boil , add the soft tofu, sundubu in large chunks and bring it to boil.
  3. Now add the noodles and pour in half of the vegetable flakes. Boil the noodles until cooked , it will take about 3 minutes.
  4. Crack an egg over the noodles , you can stir the yolk if preferred. Turn the heat off and add the scallion and a pinch of pepper.

All done ! Enjoy!
